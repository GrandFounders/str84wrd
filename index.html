Static Invoice System

A canvas-based workspace for invoices, receipts, statements, orders, transactions, and blank documents.

Products, clients, company details, and archives are stored locally in your browser unless you export or import JSON from Export in Settings.

Quick tips

  • Edit mode changes the document; Preview hides editing chrome.
  • Setup opens invoice details, clients, company, and export/counter. Use Themes for shell colors.
  • Open the menu (top right) for templates, canvas tools, and zoom.
  • Drag the canvas to pan; use the floating zoom control to zoom in or out.
